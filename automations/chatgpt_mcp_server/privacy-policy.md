# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-20

We collect and process only the data needed to run automations you ask for through this Custom GPT, including what you send in chat and what flows through any **Actions** or **connectors** you enable toward systems you control.

## What we process

- Content from connected capabilities you authorize (for example HTTP Actions targeting endpoints you configure, and any tools those systems expose).
- Operational metadata needed to fulfill requests (for example timestamps, request identifiers, and execution-related logs visible in the product).

## How we use data

- Execute the workflows and automations you request.
- Produce responses and status in the conversation.

ChatGPT and OpenAI may also process account, chat, and product data under **[OpenAI’s privacy policy](https://openai.com/privacy)**. This notice does not replace that policy.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use your data to train public models beyond what **OpenAI** describes for your plan and settings.

## Retention

- Data in third-party systems you connect depends on **those** services and your settings there.
- Data handling in **ChatGPT** follows **OpenAI** retention and controls for your account.

## Security

- Access should follow the least privilege you grant when connecting tools and Actions.
- Secrets and credentials belong in secure configuration under your control, not in shared prompts.
